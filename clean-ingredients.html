Clean Ingredients

What are clean ingredients?

Clean ingredients are simple and good for your body. This means that we put no animal by-products in any of our yummy baked goods. You don’t have to worry about dairy, eggs, honey, etc. There’s no need for those!

"But how do you make your sweets taste just as good as ‘normal’ bakeries?" We use ingredients that come from plants, such as nut milks or flax seed to take place of the animal products.

Don’t worry, we got you and your taste buds covered.

Clean Benefits

The Environment

We don’t have a second planet Earth! This is our only one, which means we have to take care of it as much as we possibly can. The United Nations stated that, “greenhouse gas footprint of animal agriculture rivals that that of every car, truck, bus, ship, airplane and rocket ship combined” (1).

Water conservation has become very important over the last decade. Humans are using more water than the Earth is able to handle. One of the most important ways that we can decrease our water intake (without not drinking or using water because we need it, duh) is by not consuming eggs and milk. Eggs and milk account for 4,285 liters of water per kilogram versus sugar crops and fruit that only use 1,159 liters of water per kilogram (2). That is almost 4 times less!

Here at Sungrown Bakery, we love our planet and hope to continue to do our best to take care of it for future generations to come.

What to watch: Cowspiracy, Before The Flood

Health

Most people are lactose intolerant. When they eat dairy they might have serve stomach pains, intestine pains, headaches, etc. People tend to feel weighed down after they consumer dairy products. Our bodies aren’t made to digest something that was meant for a baby cow. We want you to feel like your best self even after you’ve eaten a yummy dessert!

Eggs, on the other hand, are high in cholesterol. It’s as serious as a heart attack! Having a high cholesterol can lead to a higher risk of heart disease due to the build up of fatty deposits in your arteries (1). Clogged arteries can prevent your blood cells from passing through your body smoothly. Nobody wants that!

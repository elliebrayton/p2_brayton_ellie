Plant-Based Ingredients, Clear Results

What is 'Plant-Based' Ingredients

Clean ingredients are simple and good for your body. This means that we put no animal by-products in any of our yummy baked goods. You don’t have to worry about dairy, eggs, honey, etc. There’s no need for those!

"But how do you make your sweets taste just as good as ‘normal’ bakeries?" We use ingredients that come from plants, such as nut milks or flax seed to take place of the animal products.

Don’t worry, we got you and your taste buds covered.

Benefits

The Environment

Feed: Meat comes from live animals that are then killed. But before their lives are ended, they have to eat so they can grow. In order to produce one pound of beef, the cow must be fed 5.5-6.5 pounds of grain. (Source)

Water: According to the Water Footprint Network, the largest part of a consumers water footprint is indirect rather than direct. They suggest, “substitut[ing] a consumer product that has a large water footprint by a different type of product that has a smaller water footprint. Examples: eat less meat or become vegetarian, drink tea instead of coffee, or even better drink plain water.” (Source)

Greenhouse Gas Emissions:Animal agriculture is responsible for 18% of greenhouse gas emissions. That does not sound like a lot, but it is more than the exhaust from all transportation combined (Source). Livestock produce enormous amounts of methane….cow farts. While one might giggle at this, it’s no laughing matter. Methane is 25-100 times more destructive than CO2 on a 20 year time frame (Source)

What to watch: Cowspiracy, Before The Flood

Health

Carcinogens: We hear this word all the time but what does it mean? According to the American Cancer Society, “substances and exposures that can lead to cancer are called carcinogens.” In 2015 the International Agency for Research on Cancer evaluated numerous studies across 10 countries and determined that, “each 50 gram portion of processed meat eaten daily increases the risk of colorectal cancer by 18%.”(Source)

Diabetes: One study of U.S. women aged 26 to 46 years old, found that “frequent consumption of bacon, hot dogs, and sausage was each associated with an increased risk of diabetes.” They concluded that diets that were high in processed meats could increase one’s risk for developing Type 2 diabetes. (Source)

What to watch: What The Health, Forks Over Knives

Animal Rights

Pigs are so cute right? Here at Sungrown Bakery we believe that all animals (dogs, cats, pigs, chickens, cows, etc) are equal. There’s no need for one to be loved and one to be eaten. They all deserve hugs and kisses! This is why we decided to not use any animal products in our sweet treats. The moment you take a bite, the animals will be thanking you for thinking of them.

What to watch: Earthlings, Eating Animals
